Our Story

We Went Through One of These Programs.
Here Is What We Found.

Watch Our Story  ·  5 min

A few years back we enrolled in a commodity brokering program. It cost around $10,000 and promised a path into the energy market. What arrived was a collection of documents, a general overview of the industry, and an assumption that we would figure the rest out on our own. When we needed real support, it wasn't there.

A year later, we don't know a single person from that program who is still doing this. Not one. Not because the industry is impossible, but because there was never any real infrastructure behind the program. No verified product. No one actually working deals. No path to close.

We stayed. We spent the past year doing the hard work of building genuine relationships with operators, refiners, and suppliers in the Houston energy market. Learning how deals actually close. Earning trust with people who have real allocations to move.

Now we are in a position to give you what we never had -- real mentorship, access to verified product allocations from major U.S. refiners, and a real path to your first closed deal.
